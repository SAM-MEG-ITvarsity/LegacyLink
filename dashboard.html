LegacyLink

Building a Legacy, One Child at a Time

Legacy Link is a digital inheritance platform designed to help parents secure their children’s financial future — long before adulthood begins. Our mission is to make generational wealth planning simple, secure, and accessible for every family.

Through Legacy Link, parents can create inheritance funds for key life areas such as:

🏠 Housing
🎓 Education
🚗 Cars
💼 Business
💰 Other

These funds are safely managed by trusted banks and insurance partners, and can only be accessed by the child when they turn 19, ensuring financial maturity and responsibility.

We believe every child deserves to start adulthood with a strong financial foundation — and every parent deserves peace of mind knowing their legacy is protected.

Our Mission

To empower families to build generational wealth through structured, transparent, and technology-driven inheritance planning.

Our Vision

A world where every child steps into adulthood financially ready — breaking cycles of poverty and promoting lifelong stability.
